Move to the right in the world, collecting bottles and coins. In order to eliminate the chickens or the chicks, You can jump on them or throw salsa at them, but the endboss can only be eliminated by throwing salsa at it. You will need all bottles, in order to eliminate the endboss.

Once upon a time in Mexico, lived a charismatic and adventurous young man named Pepe Peligroso. One sunny afternoon, while Pepe was enjoying a delicious plate of enchiladas, the sky darkened, and thunder rumbled as a massive shadow loomed over the desert.
The shadow, outlined a monstrous fowl of enormous proportions called El Pollo Loco, hungry for destruction . It was the biggest chicken that Pepe had seen, and it had a legion of smaller chickens and adorable chicks at its side, all under its command.
Realizing the dire situation, Pepe armed himself with a collection of enchanted salsa bottles, embarking on an epic quest to defeat it. The enchanted salsa bottles had the power to unleash fiery salsa attacks, that could defeat El Pollo Loco and its chicken minions.
Would Pepe Peligroso have the courage and the spice to defeat El Pollo Loco and restore peace to the town? Only time, courage, and a lot of salsa would tell.
